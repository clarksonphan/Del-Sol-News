About Del Sol


Del Sol’s mission is to provide La Quinta’s student body with accurate and reliable information needed to function as knowledgeable citizens in a democratic society. Del Sol journalists help define their community, create common language and accessible knowledge, and push their peers beyond complacency. This is achieved by providing interesting and relevant information, reporting crises, and amplifying unheard voices.
